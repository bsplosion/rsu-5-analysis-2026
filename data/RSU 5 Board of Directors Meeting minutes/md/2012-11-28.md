8. Superintendent'S REPORT:
A. School and District Level Strategic Planning — Superintendent Welsh reported the building level
plans are a result of the Board's hard work and investments early on in the formation of the RSU.
The district Strategic Plan created during the 2009-2010 school year has becoming a living
~~ document, revised over the years and reported on annually in the State of the Schools report.
The Board created systems to support the work in the plan including teacher leadership stipends
to help lead learning and also invested in professional development time for staff.
