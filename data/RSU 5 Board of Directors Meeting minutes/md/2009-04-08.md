3. REVIEW OF RSU NO. 5 FY '10 BUDGET:

Additional budget information was provided to the Board in an attempt to further clarify budget items

and the resulting tax impact. Several Board members and citizens expressed concern about the tax

impact to Pownal. The Board asked administration to review the budget recommendations, including

those recommended by the current school boards. They would like to hear the rationale for keeping

specific items, the priorities, where budget reductions can be made, and what the impact would be of

those reductions. Administration will work with staff and provide this information at the meeting

scheduled for April 15, 2009. The goal is to make every attempt to reduce the tax impact while

maintaining a strong educational program in all our schools.
