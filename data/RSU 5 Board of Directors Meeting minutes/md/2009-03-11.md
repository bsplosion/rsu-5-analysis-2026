8. NEW BUSINESS:
A. Freedom of Access Training: Superintendents Tomaszewski and Welsh reviewed the
requirements of Maine's Freedom of Access laws relating to public records and
proceedings. The frequently asked questions were reviewed and members of the Board
completed certificates attesting that the training has been completed. Original certificates
will be kept on file in the RSU central office. Copies of certificates will be sent to
individual Board members and their respective town office.

10. BOARD COMMENTS (Reports from Sub-Committees):

A. Finance: John Morang reported the Finance Sub-Committee met on March 5°" with the
Superintendents. The warrant for transition funds was reviewed and the committee
discussed the warrant process that will be followed. The budget process and possible
presentation formats were also discussed along with a tentative schedule for the
committee to meet to review the budget presentation. The committee will meet again on
March 19" at 7:00AM and March 25" at 9:00AM.

B. Communications: Betsy Peters reported the Communications Sub-Committee met on
March 9" with the Superintendents. The committee decided on a charge: facilitating
communication between the RSU No. 5 Board and the public. This facilitation takes
many forms including recommending management of the Board's electronic
communication as well as non-electronic media. The committee's focus currently is on
the RSUS website. Immediate goals and action items were discussed. The committee
recommendations to the Board include: immediate revamp of the website; web files
created for internet broadcast and DVD for cable broadcast; sub-committees making a
report out to the board with a significant impact will submit a written report containing
the vital information to be given to the Board Chair/Superintendent for development of a
press release; e-mail system for the Board. Chair then responds with a reply to all (goal to
respond within 48 hours).

11. FINANCIAL STATEMENT FOR INTERIM BUDGET:

A. RSU No. 5 Financial Report March 5, 2009
John Morang reviewed the financial statement for RSU transitional funds. Freeport is
acting as the fiscal agent for the RSU. Two bills have been paid to the district's legal
firm. A financial statement will be provided to the full Board on a monthly basis.
