5. UNFINISHED BUSINESS:

A. Discussion of the 2017-2018 School Budget — Superintendent Foley explained an error that
was discovered on the recommended budget impact page of the handouts that were previously
shared with the Board and public. It is an amount from last year (2016-2017) that was
incorrectly carried forward into the spreadsheet. The net impact to taxation increases from
4.22% to 6.43%. It does not impact the 4.86% expenditure increase change from last year's
budget.
