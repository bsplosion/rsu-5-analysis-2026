5. WORKSHOP: PRESENTATION OF RSU NO. 5 FY'12 BUDGET:
Superintendent Welsh presented the recommended 2011-2012 RSU No. 5 budget to the Board and
public. The purpose of this initial budget presentation was to educate the Board and public about
what was contained in the recommended budget. The budget has been developed to help RSU No. 5
realize the goal of educational excellence while demonstrating a greater efficiency in the use of our
; resources. Budget priorities for FY' 12 include capital investments, technology investments,
enrollments, curriculum materials, athletics and time. Superintendent Welsh reviewed enrollments
for cach school and the tuition students projected for next year. Kelly Wentworth, Director of
Finance reviewed the budget specifics and tax impact to each town. The recommended budget shows
an overall decrease of $18,908 (-.08%) with the following impact to each community: Durham a
reduction of $22,133; Freeport a reduction of $17,612; and Pownal a reduction of $33,732.
Continued budget deliberations will occur over the next two months ending June 8, 2011 with a
three-town Annual Budget Meeting followed by a Budget Validation Referendum on June 14". All
citizens were asked to participate in the process.
