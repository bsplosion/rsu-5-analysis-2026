5. 2010-2011 BUDGET:
Superintendent Welsh reviewed the packet of information that included the following: budget
adjustments; an updated teachers per grade sheet; information on Pownal, Freeport and Durham
middle schools; and the Pownal Middle School Recommendation Summary.
A lengthy discussion took place on the recommendation in the budget to replace Pownal's current
middle school program with the choice of attending either Durham or Freeport middle schools. The
Board requested that the Pownal Parents of current 5" 6", and 7” grade students be asked which
school they would select if they had the choice of their middle school child attending school next
year in Pownal, Durham, or Freeport. Transportation would be provided to the schools. Families
will provide this information by Monday, April 26" so the Board can review the information at their
next meeting on Wednesday, April 28". The Board will then consider a vote on one of the following
options:
1. Keep all Pownal students at Pownal Middle School;
2. Eliminate the Pownal Middle School program and allow Pownal students in grades 6-8
the choice of Freeport or Durham middle school programs with transportaiotn provided;
3. Allow Pownal students in grades 6-8 the choice of attending school at Pownal, Freeport,
or Durham.
