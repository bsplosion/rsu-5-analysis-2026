6. WORKSHOP:
A. FY14 Budget Review — Opportunity for Town Leadership and Public to Comment
Chair Larkins reviewed the budget process schedule for the next two months. Tonight's meeting
is an opportunity for the Board to hear from the Pownal Selectmen on the proposed budget.
Selectmen Tim Giddinge and Al Fauver were at the meeting. Tim had met previously with
Finance Director Kelly Wentworth to review the assessment summary documents.
Board members asked questions about the budget including: valuation changes; literacy and
math strategist positions; FMS Assistant Principal/teacher leader position; professional
development time; and a priority list.
Public Comments on the Budget:
Freeport: Shari Broder asked a question about the restructuring of the FHS Alternative Ed
Program.
Pownal: Marcia Bowen asked a question about the assessment summary page in the budget
handout.
