15. BOARD COMMENTS (Reports from Sub-Committees):

. 4 A. Finance ~ John Morang reported the committee met on February 13, 2013. The finance
Director presented a draft Request for Proposals for Financial Advisory Services. The financial
advisory firm will be appointed to begin its work in the spring of 2013 to assist with the
proposed referendum election and, if approved by the voters, issuance of Bond Anticipation
Notes and the General Obligation Bonds. Proposals were due on March 8". The committee met
this afternoon to interview the firms. The Finance Committee will make its recommendation to
the RSU Board on March 27, 2013. Warrants were signed.

B. Strategic Communications — Candy Decsipkes reported the committee met to review the State
of the Schools draft report which will be mailed to homes on March 22, 2013. The committee
also talked about the Dine and Discuss sessions that will be held on April 10" in Pownal, April
24" in Durham and May 8" in Freeport. Topics at the Dine and Discuss will include the State
of the Schools report, the High School renovations and the 2013-2014 school budget.

C. Facilities Committee Update- Peter Murray reported the committee has presented to governing
boards in all three communities. The Freeport Town Council took a sentiment vote in support
of the referendum. The renovatefhs.org website has information about the renovations. There
will be a public informational meeting on April 3, 2013 in the Freeport High School cafe.
