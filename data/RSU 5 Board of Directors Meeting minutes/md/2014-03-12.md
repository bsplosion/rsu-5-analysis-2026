5. Superintendent'S REPORT:

A. Withdrawal Update - There have been on-going meetings of the Working Group and a joint
meeting with the Freeport Withdrawal Committee. These will continue and additional updates
will be provided at the next Board meeting.

B. Parent Group Budget Meetings Update - The Superintendent has been meeting with parent
groups from our schools. The information gained from these meetings will be incorporated into
the FY15 budget presentation at the end of March.

B. State of the Schools Overview

7. UNFINISHED BUSINESS:

A. Discussion on Superintendent Search — The Board decided to use the following criteria to hire
an interim Superintendent for 2014-2015: experience as a Superintendent; commitment to a full
year position; and recent interim or active work as a Superintendent. Maine School Management
keeps an updated list of candidates interested in interim positions. The Superintendent, Chair
and Vice-Chair will discuss with Maine School Management viable candidates and develop a
list of people to interview. The Board will interview candidates to determine the top candidate.
The Chair and Vice-Chair will then negotiate a contract within parameters set by the Board.
