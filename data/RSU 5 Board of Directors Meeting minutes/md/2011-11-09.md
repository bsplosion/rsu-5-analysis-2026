5. WORKSHOP:

A. Use of Technology: Seth Thompson, Technology Director, presented on what we've done and
where we are going with technology in the RSU. Last school year the following was accomplished:
purchased laptops for all elementary teachers; redefined technology roles to streamline systems
support and classroom support; increased computer technician staffing to 2 full-time employees and
1 school year employee; selected PowerSchool as the student information system for the district;
and replaced two wireless networks, four network file servers and various network equipment.
Priorities for next year include: 6" grade laptops at DCS and FMS: MSS and PES computer labs;
sound amplification system; mounted classroom LCD projectors; and professional development
time.

Laura Girr, Freeport High School Technology Integrator reported on how teachers are using
technology in their teaching. This is the 8" year with the laptops. Shawn Favreau, Freeport Middle
School Social Studies Teacher reported on the work students are doing with technology.
