Annual Budget Meeting
May 26, 2010
Freeport High School Gymnasium
Superintendent Welsh officially called to order the Annual Budget Meeting for Regional School Unit
No. 5 at 6:35 P.M.
Chair Larkins reminded those in attendance to pick up the handouts on the table in the foyer, which
include figures, and explanations on the budget. Chair Larkins also reminded everyone the next step in
the budget process is the June 8, 2010 referendum. Absentee ballots will be available on May 27, 2010
Superintendent Welsh mentioned over the past year the Board has adopted a Vision, Mission and
Strategic Framework for the RSU. A student prepared video was shown on what students love about the
education they received at Freeport High School.
Article 1A: Board Chair Larkins nominated James G. Boyles for Moderator. Board member Dube
seconded the nomination.

No further nominations occurred and Mr. Boyles was elected by unanimous vote.
James G. Boyles was sworn in by Superintendent Welsh.
Mr. Boyles explained non-members of the community could only speak if given permission by members
present. Mr. Boyles asked for unanimous consent that the following non-resident Administrators:
Cheryl White, Will Pidden, Ryan Gleason, Joan Nason, Russell Packett, Dennis Ouellette, Sarah
Simmonds be allowed to speak. There being no objection it was so ordered.
Mr. Boyles explained some process/rules for the meeting:

- A person may not speak before being recognized by the moderator;

- This is not an open discussion meeting; a motion or amendment must be on the floor before

discussion takes place;

- Speakers must come to the microphone and state their name and town of residence.
