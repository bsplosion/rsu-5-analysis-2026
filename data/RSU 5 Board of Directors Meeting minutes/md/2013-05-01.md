6. WORKSHOP:

A. FY14 Budget Review — Opportunity for Town Leadership and Public to Comment
Chair Larkins thanked everyone for attending the Dine and Discuss. Tonight's meeting is an
opportunity for the Board to hear from the Freeport Town Councilors on the proposed budget.
Councilors Melanie Sachs and Sarah Tracy asked about the plan for the alternative education
students at the high school, and charter school funds in the budget.

Public Comments on the Budget:

Freeport: Kristen Dorsey asked about the alternative education program, class sizes at the
schools and capital projects.

Nancy Drolet spoke of concerns with the alternative education program changes.

Marie Gunning spoke of concerns with cuts to the budget and safety issues at Morse Street
School.

Lindsay Sterling asked about the State grading system and staff being reclassified to different
areas.
