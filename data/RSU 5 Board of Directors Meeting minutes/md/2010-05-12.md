Article 15: VOTED: (21) To see what sum the Regional School Unit will raise and
appropriate in additional local funds (Recommend $2,965,424.00), which exceeds the
State's Essential Programs and Services allocation model by (Recommend
$2,537,323.00) as required to fund the budget recommended by the Regional School Unit
Board.
The Regional School Unit Board recommends $2,965,424.00 for additional local funds
and gives the following reasons for exceeding the State's Essential Programs and
Services funding model by $2,537,323.00: The State's Essential Program and
Services funding formula is a minimum model to support education in grades K-12.
RSU No. 5 provides additional funds to support lower student / teacher ratios,
additional co-curricular activities in addition to complete educational programs for
all our students.
Explanation: The additional local funds are those locally raised funds over and above
the Regional School Unit's local contribution to the total cost of funding public education
from kindergarten to grade 12 as described in the Essential Programs and Services
Funding Act and local amounts raised for the annual payment on non-state funded debt
service that will help achieve the Regional School Unit budget for educational programs.
(Dube — Holt) (9 — 1 Parker)
