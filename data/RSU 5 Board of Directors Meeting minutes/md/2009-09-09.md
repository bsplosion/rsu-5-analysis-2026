3. ADJUSTMENTS TO THE AGENDA:
Addition of 3.5 A. regarding the new Durham school project.
Addition of 6. Old Business updates
3.5
A. Voted: (1) That the new Durham school be named the Durham Community School.
(Holt — Kaplan) (11 — 0)
4. WORKSHOP:
David Ruff and Duke Albanese, Co-Executive Directors of the Great Schools Partnership, were in
attendance to present the data that will be distributed at the Community Forum on Monday,
September 14, 2009. The agenda and process for the forum was also reviewed. After the program
in the FPAC group discussions will take place around the areas of Innovation, Financial Efficiency
and Academics. After the forum Great Schools will compile the information from the group
discussions to present to the Board on October 14, 2009. The Board will use the information to
create a three-year strategic plan. A second community forum will be held at the end of October for
additional community input.
5. PERSONNEL:
A. Voted: (2) To employ Sarah Simmonds as Curriculum Director for the RSU for the 2009-2010
school year. (Parker — Poissonnier) (11 — 0)
B. Voted: (3) To employ Jennifer Kelly as a Literacy Strategist for the RSU for the 2009-2010
school year. (Poissonnier — Parker) (11 — 0)
6. OLD BUSINESS:
A. Beth Parker mentioned Freeport Recreation and Community Education is looking for
representatives from Pownal and Durham to join the Advisory Committee. The meetings are
held the first Wednesday of the month.
