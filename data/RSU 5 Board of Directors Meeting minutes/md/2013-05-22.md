Annual Budget Meeting
May 22, 2013
Freeport High School Gymnasium
Co Superintendent Welsh officially called to order the Annual Budget Meeting for Regional School Unit
Article 1A: | Board Chair Mr. Larkins nominated Mr. Gregory Im for Moderator. Board member
Mr. Murray seconded the nomination. No further nominations occurred and Mr. Im was
elected by unanimous vote. Gregory Im was sworn in by Superintendent Welsh.
Pledge of Allegiance
Mr. Im thanked everyone for coming to the meeting and explained the following:
-The 5 Ballot clerks have been sworn in. Voting attendees should have a yellow voting card.
-The meeting will follow the Maine Moderators Manual with a few modifications as follows.
- Mr. Im allowed the following administrators in RSUS who live outside Durham, Freeport or
Pownal be allowed to answer questions at this meeting: Tom Ambrose, Ann Marie Barter, Lisa
Demick, Will Pidden, Ryan Gleason, Seth Thompson, Dennis Ouellette, Dominic DePatsy and
Joe Makley. There were no objections
- As needed debate will be limited to 2 minutes per article.
- To avoid confusion there will be no amendments to amendments to motions.
- By law Articles 12, 13 and 14 require recorded votes.
- By law Article 15 requires a written ballot
There were no objections to the modifications to the Maine Moderators Guide
Ground rules on speaking:
-Come to microphone in the center to speak.
-State your name, the town you are from and what your purpose for speaking.
-No generalized speeches
-The bond for renovations to Freeport High School is separate from the school budget we are discussing
tonight.
-Questions are addressed to the Moderator.
-If you wish to speak, line up at the microphone.
-[f you wish to end debate on a motion say, “I move the previous question”.
-The meeting will vote by raising the yellow voting cards and say Aye or Nay
