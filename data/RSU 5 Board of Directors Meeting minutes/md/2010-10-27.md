7. REPORTS FROM ADMINISTRATORS:

A. Strategic Framework Action Plans: Superintendent Welsh reviewed work to date on the Strategic
Plan. The October 8" Professional Learning Day was the kick-off of the Strategic Plan. We
walked through the plan with staff. Staff then returned to their buildings and developed Action
Plans with three questions in mind: 1) What does this mean for your building; 2) What does this
mean for your team; 3) What does this mean for you as an individual. The goal of the work on
the plans is to improve our ability to analyze student data and use it to make instructional and
curriculum decisions. In March we will have data from the first 6 months, and by May a plan for
a full year. Staff will continue this work during the next Professional Learning Day on
November 12",

14. BOARD COMMENTS (Reports from Sub-Committees):

A. Finance — Chair Eric Dube reported the committee met on October 13, 2010. To date
approximately 24 of the expected tuition students are currently attending Freeport High School
making enrollment approximately 500. This is creating a savings of about $10,000 per student.
Currently there is a shortage of about 50 lockers. The enrollment in 2011-12 is expected to be
over 550. The finance committee approved the purchase of 100 lockers for the High School. The
issues with lunch have smoothed out as some changes have been made to accommodate the
larger numbers. We may need to look at additional teachers for 2010-2011. Dennis Ouellette
Director of Transportation and Facilities went over the Strategic Plan Efficiencies completed to
date. The Durham Community School will be utilizing the Geothermal Heating/Cooling and the
installation of Natural Gas will be complete this fall at Pownal Elementary School, Morse Street
School, and the High School. Warrants were signed.

B. Communications — Chair Jane Blais reported the committee met on September 16" and October
14". The committee reviewed work done to the district and school websites during the summer
months. The committee also talked about communications with Parent Groups and Municipal
Officials. The purpose is to effectively communicate with parents and the larger community so
