1. CALLED TO ORDER:
Chair Michelle Ritcheson called the meeting to order at 6:37 p.m.

2. MEMBERS PRESENT: Kathryn Brown (arrived at 6:38 p.m.), Jeremy Clough (left at 6:45 p.m.),
Candace deCsipkes, Jennifer Galletta, Naomi Ledbetter, John Morang, Michelle Ritcheson, Lindsay

3. PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE:

4. ANNOUNCEMENTS:

6:30 p.m.

5. EXECUTIVE SESSION:

A. VOTED: To enter into Executive Session as outlined in 1 M.R.S.A § 405(6)(A) for the purpose
of discussing the Superintendent's evaluation. (Ledbetter — Sterling) (9 — 0)
Time In: 6:38 p.m. Time Out: 7:53 p.m.

6. ACTION AS A RESULT OF EXECUTIVE SESSION:

A. VOTED: To extend the Superintendent's contract two years, until 2021, increase the salary 4%
for the 2018-2019 school year beginning July 1, 2018 and increase the annuity by $1,000.
(Vertenten — Ledbetter) (9 — 0)
