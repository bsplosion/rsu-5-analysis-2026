7. OLD BUSINESS:
The Board listened to public comment and deliberated over reductions to the budget. The Board
made reductions to the budget in the amount of $85,831. The reductions include: .5 teaching
position at Freeport High School; .4 position from the Business Office; and the reduction in the
salaries for the Curriculum Director and Middle School Principal due to personnel changes. The total
overall budget reductions total $755,581. The Board adopted budget will be presented to registered
voters from Durham, Freeport, and Pownal at the Annual Budget Meeting scheduled for July 21,
2009 at 6:00 p.m. at Freeport High School. The budget validation referendum will be held on July
28, 2009 in each town.

13. BOARD COMMENTS (Reports from Sub-Committees):

A. Communications: Betsy Peters reported the committee met on July 1“ at Morse Street School.
The committee reviewed the strengths and weakness of our messaging an communications plan
for the first round of the budget. For this next budget round signs will be distinct and visible in
key locations in all three towns. Volunteers are needed to help make signs, contact Kristen
Dorsey if you would like to help. The website home page will be streamlined and begin to focus
only on next milestones for budget process, community feedback on it and documents supporting
it. Two e-mails will be sent out to addresses that are forwarded to RSUS, the first will focus on
attendance at the Annual Meeting and the second on voting for the BVR. Two Robo calls will
also be placed focusing on attendance at the Annual Meeting and voting for the BVR. Town
organizations are encouraged to provide phone numbers to reach the largest number of citizens
possible. The committee is also looking into the cost and projected effectiveness of placing ads
in local papers. Laurie Poissonnier suggested mailing flyers to citizens in all three towns. The
Committee also continued their discussion on the Future Search process. The suggestion was
made to focus less on vision and more on involving the public more directly in the strategic
planning process. The next meeting of the Communications Sub-Committee will be held at
4:30PM on July 22" at Morse Street School.

C. Finance: John Morang reported the committee met on July 8" at Morse Street School. The
committee reviewed and signed the RSU warrant and Durham construction requisitions. The
committee also discussed the budget. The next meeting of the Finance Sub-Committee will be
held on July 21" at 8:00AM.
