5. WORKSHOP: FY13 BUDGET REVIEW:

A. The workshop provided time for the Board to deliberate items on the Administrator priority list,
items community members have requested, any new proposals by Board members and the
overall budget. After much deliberation Board members indicated support by straw poll for
adding a Gifted and Talented Teacher (for one year only), reinstating the Speech Language
teacher position, funding for the FHS Concept Drawings and communications, and reinstating
4 Literacy Strategist.

Director Dennis Ouellette reported the Freeport Town Council gave the go ahead on the proposal
for RSUS to maintain the Hunter Road fields. The cost will be added to the budget but will be
offset with the revenue from the Town of Freeport.

Director Russell Packett reported the Town of Freeport will not be utilizing his department' s
services for scheduling of the Hunter Road fields. Mr. Packett also reported he will not know
until May 15" if the Town will provide financial support for the Teen Center.

The Board will vote on the budget at their meeting on May 9, 2012.
