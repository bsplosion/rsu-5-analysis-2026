5. WORKSHOP: 2010-2011 BUDGET:
Superintendent Welsh reviewed the updated handouts based on meeting discussions and
additional information received between the March 9" meeting and tonight. Additions,
reductions and revenues were reviewed. The budget does include the addition of a .8 FTE
Gifted and Talented Teacher. The tax impact to communities would be as follows: reduction
for Durham in the amount of $69,261; reduction for Freeport of $36,145; and a reduction for
Pownal of $19,474. The Board reviewed the budget by Article with discussion on items
within the Articles. The budget has a net proposed increase of .96%
The Board will meet on May 12" for further discussion and adoption of the budget. The
Annual Budget Meeting is scheduled for May 26'" and the Budget Validation Referendum is
June 8"
