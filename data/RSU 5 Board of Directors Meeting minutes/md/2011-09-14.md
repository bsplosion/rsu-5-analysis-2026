5. WORKSHOP:
A. Goal Setting Linked to Board Self-Evaluation
The Board reviewed the results of the self-evaluation. The overall rating of 80% reflects a
highly effective Board who just completed a very successful year. The seven dimensions on the
evaluation were contextual 81%, educational 78%, finance 81%, interpersonal 84%, analytical
76%, political 78% and strategic 78%. The Board discussed the following three goals for the
2011-2012 school year: to effectively communicate with parents and the larger community so
that they understand the engage in actively supporting the education of our students; to provide
appropriate Board training and support opportunities for members to “develop and reinforce the
skills required for effective policy-making, budget planning, and communications'; and to
provide leadership for securing support for the district budget with a specific plan for explaining
and defending the budget to the public.
The Board will vote on the goals at their September 28, 2011 meeting.
