3. PRESENTATION OF RSU NO. 5 FY 710 BUDGET:
Superintendent Welsh presented the proposed 2009-2010 RSU 5 Budget to the Board. The proposed
budget contained the recommended budgets from each of the current school departments. In
addition, the budget contained items that had previously been paid for by Freeport citizens as part of
the municipal budget. These items include debt and capital improvement project payments. Another
new item to the budget is the interest payment for Durham's new elementary school. The State will
give the RSU $725,000 for the interest payment and the RSU must make the payment to the Bond
Bank. This requires an increase in the budget that is offset by the increased State subsidy. The State
requires the RSU to report the interest payment in the budget.
The purpose of this initial budget presentation was to educate the Board and public about what was
contained in the recommended budgets from each current school department. Continued budget
deliberations will occur over the next two months ending in late May with a three-town Annual
Budget Meeting followed by a Budget Validation Referendum. The Board encouraged all citizens to
participate in the process.
