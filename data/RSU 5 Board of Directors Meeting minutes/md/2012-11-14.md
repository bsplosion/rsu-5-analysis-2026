7. WORKSHOP:

A. Use of Technology — Seth Thompson, Technology Director reviewed the three year technology
investment and standardization plan. In year one MLTI laptops were purchased for elementary
teachers. In year two we patched our 6" grade 1:1 program and replaced two computer labs. In
year three the focus will be classroom audio and video and the MLTI renewal. The potential
impact to the budget for year three would be between $15,000-$20,000. Variables would be
enrollment, MLTI per seat cost. Includes replacement cycle for all teachers and 6 - 12 students.
Audio and Video investment district wide.

Stacey Alvarez, Freeport Middle School teacher talked about teaching and learning ina 1:1
classroom and the importance of not just having the laptops but using them and using them
effectively. Some of the programs students use with their laptops include: Khan Academy,
SketchUp, Timeline Maker, SmartMusic, and GeoGebra. Jeff Mao, Maine DOE Learning
Technology Policy Director talked about the MLTI program which is currently in its 11" year.
