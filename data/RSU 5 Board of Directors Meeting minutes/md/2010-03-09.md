5. WORKSHOP: PRESENTATION OF RSU NO. 5 FY'11 BUDGET:
Superintendent Welsh presented the recommended 2010-2011 RSU No. 5 budget to the
Board and public. The purpose of this initial budget presentation was to educate the Board
and public about what was contained in the recommended budget. The budget has been
developed to help RSU No. 5 realize the goal of educational excellence while demonstrating
a greater efficiency in the use of our resources. The recommended budget with stimulus
funds, Adult Education and without debt service shows an overall decrease of $596,995
(2.7%). The decrease is due mainly to tuition and restructuring. Superintendent Welsh
reviewed the eleven articles in the budget and the changes made to each. The projected tax
impact change, due to offsetting local revenues, would result in a reduction for Durham and
Pownal and a small increase to Freeport.
Continued budget deliberations will occur over the next two months ending May 26" with a
three-town Annual Budget Meeting followed by a Budget Validation Referendum on June
8". All citizens were asked to participate in the process.
