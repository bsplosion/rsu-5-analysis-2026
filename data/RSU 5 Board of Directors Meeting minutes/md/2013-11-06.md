5. GOOD NEWS & RECOGNITION: .
A. Durham Teacher Update — Teachers Deb Bartlett, Patsy Smith, Nick Lyons, Jackie Arellano
and Eliza Damone shared what they have been doing with students using new technology
available to them this year. Teachers have been increasing their use of strategies that
incorporate technology into their teaching and learning to enhance students 21‘ Century
learning skills. Teachers demonstrated some of the applications they are using with students.

7. WORKSHOP:

A. Standards-based Systems: Overview & Elementary Program — Curriculum Director Joe Makley
presented the first in a series of workshops on standards. Mr. Makley explained standards are
used to clarify the focus of instruction; a framework of standards states more clearly what must
be learned, with greater specificity. A school is fully “standards-based” when it permits students
to advance to more difficult work based on their demonstrating proficiency in

B. Use of Technology — Technology Director Seth Thompson reported the District Technology
