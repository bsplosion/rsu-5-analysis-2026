5. ADJUSTMENTS TO THE AGENDA:
6. GOOD NEWS & RECOGNITION:

A. Report from Student Government — Emily Sturtevant reported on the following happenings at
Freeport High School: all fall sports have ended with the Cross Country Team making it to New
Englands; the Musical was held last week; Mock Trial made it to the quarter finals; Guidance
department held a meeting on Gap Year Semester Program; and 40 students attended training
with Boys to Men.

B. Freeport High School Student/Teacher Update — Social Studies Teacher Charlie Mellon gave an
overview of how they are identifying and implementing standards into the American Studies
curriculum. This is the first year the teachers started implementing their first Standards-based
assessment. Two of Mr. Mellon's American Studies students, Abby Gray and Margo Ruby
explained how they demonstrated their understanding of the unit. Abby wrote a poem and Ruby
created a book.

7. REPORTS FROM ADMINISTRATORS:
8. Superintendent'S REPORT:

A. Freeport High School Renovation Update: Superintendent Welsh noted the renovations will
provide students with individual work areas the two students, Abby and Margo, talked about in
their presentation. Superintendent Welsh also reported that questions have been asked about the
timeline for the renovation project once approved to spend the bond money. The steps include:
creating a building committee charge and committee; creating the RFP for an architect and
engineering firm; then 9 months for them to create drawings; construction to begin next
October/November; and full project completed September 2016.
