6. GOOD NEWS & RECOGNITION:

A. Report from Student Government: Maya Pierce and Cassie Parker reported on the following
happenings at Freeport High School: Senior Project presentations will be held June 2-5; AP
exams were last week; A third quarter student recognition assembly was held; Student Council
and Student Government elections; Prom is May 31%; and Model UN.

B. Mast Landing Grade 4 Odyssey of the Mind State Championship Recognition — Students were
recognized for winning the State Championship. Students explained what their project was and
how they.

C. Meridian Stories — Freeport Middle School students were recognized for their success in the
Meridian Stories competition. The 8" grade team created a movie trailer and the 7" grade team's
theme was food waste.

D, Freeport Middle School Student/Teacher Update: Maya Egan and Ben Berry explained how
they created chapters using an iBook for the 8" grade Dirty Thirties project.

10. ORGANIZATIONAL BUSINESS
11. UNFINISHED BUSINESS:

12. NEW BUSINESS:

A. VOTED: (3) That the warrant for the Regional School Unit No. 5 (the “Regional School Unit”)
Budget Meeting presented to the meeting be and is hereby approved and that a Regional School
Unit budget meeting be and is hereby called for May 28, 2014 for the purpose of voting on the
annual budget for the Regional School Unit for the 2014-2015 fiscal year.

(Murray - Parker) (11 — 0)

B. VOTED: (4) That the Warrant and Notice of Election for the Regional School Unit Budget
Validation Referendum presented to the meeting be and is hereby approved, and that a Regional
School Unit budget validation referendum be and is hereby called for June 10, 2014 for the
purpose of approving the budget adopted at the Regional School Unit budget meeting for the 2014-
2015 fiscal year. (Morang - Parker) (11 — 0)

C. VOTED: (5) That the form of Notice of Amounts Adopted at Budget Meeting presented to this
meeting be and is hereby approved, and that the Superintendent of Schools of the Regional School
Unit is hereby authorized and directed to complete said Notice by adding the amounts approved by
the voters for each expenditure category and the total school budget summary expenditure amount,
all in accordance with the Regional School Unit budget meeting on May 28, 2014, and to cause
copies of said notice, as completed, to be delivered to the municipal clerks of each municipality of
the Regional School Unit for posting at the polling places for the June 10, 2014 Regional School
Unit budget validation referendum. (Ritcheson - Parker) (11 — 0)

D. VOTED: (6) Consideration of action that pursuant to section 1485(4) of Title 20-A, the Finance
Committee be authorized to transfer not more than 5% of the total appropriation for any cost center
in the FY15 operating budget to another cost center or among other cost centers, provided that the
total FY15 fiscal year operating budget shall not be increased by such transfers.

(Ritcheson - Ledbetter) (11 — 0)
