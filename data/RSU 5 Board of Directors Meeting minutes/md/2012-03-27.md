5. WORKSHOP: PRESENTATION OF RSU NO. 5 FY'13 BUDGET:
Superintendent Welsh presented the recommended 2012-2013 RSU No. 5 budget to the Board and
public. The purpose of this initial budget presentation was to educate the Board and public about
what was contained in the recommended budget. The budget has been developed to help RSU No. 5
realize the goal of educational excellence while demonstrating a greater efficiency in the use of our
resources. Budget priorities for FY' 13 include capital investments, technology investments,
instructional programs/enrollments, curriculum materials, and time. Superintendent Welsh reviewed
enrollments for each school and the tuition students projected for next year. Kelly Wentworth,
Director of Finance reviewed the budget specifics and tax impact to each town. The recommended
budget reflects a net increase of 1.1% ($268,088) with the following anticipated impact to each
community:
Durham — an increase of ~ $118,020 or 3.79%
Freeport — an increase of — $210,055 or 1.58% .
Pownal — an increase of — $63,539 or 3.81%
Continued budget deliberations will occur over the next two months ending May 23, 2012 with a
three-town Annual Budget Meeting followed by a Budget Validation Referendum on June 12", All
citizens were asked to participate in the process.
