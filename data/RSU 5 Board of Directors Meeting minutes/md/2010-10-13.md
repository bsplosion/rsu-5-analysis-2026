5. WORKSHOP:
A. Energy Efficiencies and Capital Planning:
Through the Strategic Plan, a commitment was made to explore and implement a range of
strategies to support energy efficiency and cost savings. Dennis Ouellette, Director of Facilities
and Transportation presented detailed information regarding these improvements that range from
conversion to natural gas in 3 of our buildings to hot water heater and light replacements. The
savings from natural gas is anticipated to be more than $35,159 annually.
Dennis Ouellette also presented updated information from the district's Capital Plan. Projects
completed incude: Pownal School entry enclosure; Pownal gym lighting upgrade; Pownal front
roof section; and Morse Street School electric water heater replaced with natural gas. Dennis
also reported on anticipated 2011-2012 Capital budget items.
