7. REPORTS FROM Superintendent AND ADMINISTRATORS:

A. Freeport Withdrawal Vote — Attorney Dick Spencer from Drummond Woodsum spoke about the

Board's role and responsibilities in the withdrawal process. Mr. Spencer has been involved with

other withdrawals in the state and has experience with the process. Mr. Spencer spoke and answered

questions about the following: Freeport representation on the Withdrawal Committee; Board
appointed working group; withdrawal plan is an agreement between the Board and withdrawal
committee; high school education for students from Durham and Pownal; timeline of a June and/or
November referendum vote.

The Board will look at creating a working group with representatives from Durham and Pownal to
represent the Board in negotiations.

B. Enrollments — Superintendent Welsh reported last year there was an unexpected 3% increase in
enrollments due to families moving into housing in Freeport. This year there is a decrease in
enrollments across all the schools which brings us close to enrollments in 2011-2012. The majority
