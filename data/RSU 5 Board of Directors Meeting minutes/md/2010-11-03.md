5. WORKSHOP:
A. Use of Technology:
The Strategic Framework includes an academic program commitment to use technology
effectively to enhance learning. The workshop focused on the exemplary use of technology as
an instructional tool showcasing the new technology available at the Durham Community
School. Principal Will Pidden showed a video of technology use by students in the classroom.
Information was provided by Technology Director Seth Thompson on our current hardware and
support; our identified needs; and a long-term implementation plan to bring us closer to the goal
of having exemplary use in each of our schools. Projected financials were provided for the
Board's consideration as part of our on-going budget priorities discussion.
Board members discussed having dinner and attending the movie “Waiting for Superman”.
Members were reminded to discuss education in general but nothing specific to RSU 5.
