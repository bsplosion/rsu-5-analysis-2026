7. REPORTS FROM ADMINISTRATORS:

A. Update on the Campus Complex Fundraising Committee Work — Craig Sickels provided the
Board with an update on the work of the committee. The fundraising efforts will kick-off with a
reception on May 31” at the Hilton Garden. There will be naming opportunities and the
potential to receive some grant money. Additional informational meetings will be held in each
community.

8. Superintendent'S REPORT:

A. Teacher Leader Stipends — Superintendent Welsh reported the Strategic Framework includes an
academic program commitment to leadership development at all levels within the organization.
In October the Board approved teacher leadership stipends to create teams in each building that
met the unique needs of individual buildings while also supporting our district level work
focused on teaching and learning. This spring, we asked for teacher feedback on the structure
and nature of the leadership responsibilities and we received very positive feedback. No
changes were recommended for next year.

15. BOARD COMMENTS (Reports from Sub-Committees):

A. Finance — Eric Dube reported the committee met on May 11, 2011. Kelly Wentworth
distributed copies of the FY 2009/2010 financial statements received from our auditors. The
budget was discussed and no new changes were being proposed to the recommended budget
since the April 27" meeting. Currently the communities of Durham and Freeport will see a 1%
tax increase while Pownal remains flat. The Finance Committee meeting schedule for 201 1-
2012 was discussed and tentatively approved. The next meeting is scheduled for June 8, 2011.

B. Negotiations — Nelson Larkins reported support staff negotiations are ongoing; Administrator
negotiations are close to being finalized; and teachers are in mediation.
