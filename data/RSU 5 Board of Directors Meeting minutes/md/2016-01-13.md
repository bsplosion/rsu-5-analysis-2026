Mr. Palmer and Mr. Paterson updated the Board on their fundraising efforts to date and asked

Mr. Keck reported on the progress on the project. Some unsuitable soil has been removed and
replaced. The sewer pipe work has been completed. Steel will be erected by Valentine's Day.
