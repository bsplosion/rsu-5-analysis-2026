A. VOTED: That the warrant for the Regional School Unit No. 5 (the “Regional School Unit”)

B. VOTED: That the Warrant and Notice of Election for the Regional Schoo] Unit Budget

places for the June 11, 2019 Regional School Unit budget validation referendum.
