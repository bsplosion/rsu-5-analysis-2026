RSU No. 5 Board of Directors Meeting
Wednesday, May 27, 2015 — 5:30 p.m.
Freeport High School - Gymnasium
Meeting Minutes
(NOTE: These Minutes are not official until approved by the Board of Directors. Such action, either to
approve or amend and approve, is anticipated at the June 10, 2015 meeting).
CALLED TO ORDER:
Chairman Nelson Larkins called the meeting to order at 5:35 p.m.
MEMBERS PRESENT: Kathryn Brown (arrived at 5:43 p.m.), Candace Decsipkes, Nelson Larkins,
Naomi Ledbetter, John Morang, Beth Parker, Brian Pike, Michelle Ritcheson,
Lindsay Sterling, Valeria Steverlynck, Karin VanNostrand
MEMBERS ABSENT:
3. PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE:
A motion was made by Steverlynck, seconded by Sterling to adjust the agenda to include a brief
discussion on how the Board, together with the Superintendents, will publicize, promote, and support the
budget approved by the citizens at tonight's Annual Budget Meeting. Discussion to take place at the end
of the agenda. VOTED: (1) (10 — 0)
4. NEW BUSINESS:
A. VOTED: (2) To authorize, subject to negotiations, maintenance personnel to join the Coastal
Education Association Educational Support Professional Unit.

evidence of a majority wanting to become a member of the Educational Support Professional
Unit. (Steverlynck — Sterling) (10 - 0)

ADJUSTMENT TO THE AGENDA:
Moved by Steverlynck, seconded by Sterling to publicize, promote, and support the budget approved by
the citizens at tonight's Annual Budget Meeting
Amendment #1: Moved by Steverlynck, seconded by Sterling to include information dissemination
and the best explanation possible that we can provide to the public without taking a for or against
position and explain what the budget drivers are.
Amendment to Amendment #1: Moved by Sterling, seconded by Steverlynck to allow the
Strategic Communications Committee, and anyone else available, to meet in the next couple days to -
come up with a more informational explanation, in common terms, that would allow taxpayers to
understand, and provide that information to the papers by Friday or Saturday morning. (11 — 0)
Amendment to Amendment#1: Moved by Brown, seconded by Steverlynck that prior to sending
the information to the papers, it will be sent to each Board member for review first. (11-0)
Vote on Amendment #1 11 —0
