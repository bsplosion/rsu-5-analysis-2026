6. WORKSHOP:

A. Energy Efficiencies and Capital Planning - Dennis Ouellette, Facilities and Transportation
Director reviewed Capital Budget projects completed in 2013 including: purchase of a
handicapped equipped bus; maintenance truck and sander; Pownal and Morse Street
carpeting; Morse Street and Middle School roofs; trim work at Pownal; Mast Landing
playground; construction of Middle School Choices and Life Skills rooms; wall repair at
Pownal; and basement and wall repairs at central office. The district has seen a savings due
to energy efficiencies from the conversion to natural gas. The total cost avoidance 2010-2013
is $464,514.

Dennis Ouellette also reviewed the 2014-2015 anticipated Capital Budget. Each year Facilities
Committee members tour the schools and review requests from the Principals to rebuild the Five
Year Capital Plan and reprioritize. Items for 2014-2015 were discussed both if the FHS
Renovation Referendum passes and if it fails.

B. Durham, Freeport, Pownal Municipal Leadership - Selectmen/Town Council and Budget
Advisory members from our three towns were invited to the meeting with a goal of
increasing communication and building relationships. Those in attendance broke out into
small groups to discuss their thoughts, questions or concerns about topics of importance to

the three towns and the RSU. Board members reported out on conversations in each group.
Topics of discussion included: the FHS renovation bond; issues of the RSU and benefits of
the RSU; rotating Board meetings between the three communities; changing the name of
Freeport High School; the importance of communication; explaining numbers to town
