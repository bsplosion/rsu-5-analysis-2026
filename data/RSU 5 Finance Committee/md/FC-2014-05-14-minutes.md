Agenda Items and Discussion:
Budget Review:
Finance Director reviewed the revised Budget Impact sheet which reflects the additions/
reduction made at the May 7, 2014 Board Meeting. Kelly also shared the cost impact per median
household spreadsheet, which reflects a proposed annual increase for the median taxpayer in
each community to be: $161.45 in Durham; $130.46 in Freeport; and $217.67 in Pownal based
on April 1, 2013 town taxable valuations, median taxpayer, and mil rates.
Review of Draft 2014/2015 Warrant & Meeting Schedule:
Finance Director presented the draft versions of the 2014/2015 Finance Meeting Schedule and
Warrant Schedule. Meeting schedule is subject to change based on the Board of Director’s
adopted schedule. The Finance Committee agreed to meet on the evening of the first monthly
Board meeting. Updated schedules will be presented for review at the June 11, 2014 meeting.
