1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

RSU 5 is soliciting Statements of Qualifications from qualified architectural and planning firms to
develop a Comprehensive Facility Study and 10-Year Long-Range Master Plan. This project will
synchronize physical infrastructure and transportation logistics with modern educational needs to
ensure a sustainable future for the communities of Freeport, Durham, and Pownal.
