# REQUEST FOR QUALIFICATIONS (RFQ)

## Comprehensive Facility Study & 10-Year Long-Range Master Plan

**Regional School Unit 5 (RSU 5)**
Freeport – Durham – Pownal, Maine

---

### 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

RSU 5 is soliciting Statements of Qualifications from qualified architectural and planning firms to develop a Comprehensive Facility Study and 10-Year Long-Range Master Plan. This project will synchronize physical infrastructure and transportation logistics with modern educational needs to ensure a sustainable future for the communities of Freeport, Durham, and Pownal.

### 2. FACILITY INVENTORY

The study must encompass the following eight (8) locations:

- **Central Office:** 17 West St, Freeport, ME (Administration & Community Programs)
- **Transportation Facility:** Currently leasing space (Maintenance & Fleet Storage)
- **Morse Street School:** 21 Morse St, Freeport, ME (Grades PreK–2)
- **Mast Landing School:** 20 Mollymauk Lane, Freeport, ME (Grades 3–5)
- **Freeport Middle School:** 19 Kendall Lane, Freeport, ME (Grades 6–8)
- **Freeport High School:** 30 Holbrook St, Freeport, ME (Grades 9–12)
- **Durham Community School:** 654 Hallowell Rd, Durham, ME (Grades PreK–8)
- **Pownal Elementary School:** 587 Elmwood Rd, Pownal, ME (Grades PreK–5)

### 3. SCOPE OF SERVICES & GOALS

The selected firm will be responsible for:

- **Condition & Educational Analysis:** Assess existing conditions and educational needs for each school.
- **10-Year Sustainable Plan:** Provide a sustainable approach to facility and educational needs over a decade.
- **Demographics & Enrollment Projections:** Develop demographic projections and alternative district-wide configurations.
- **Transportation Logistics:** Evaluate the Transportation Facility and bus storage for capacity, safety, and future fleet electrification.
- **Space Reuse:** Prepare descriptions of possible building space reuse to economically meet future community needs.
- **Community Engagement:** Solicit feedback and explore responsive solutions through active community dialogue.
- **Prioritization:** Rank improvements based on conditions, current laws, future space needs, and code requirements.

### 4. BUDGETARY COST ESTIMATES

For each proposed configuration alternative, the Consultant shall provide:

- **Total Project Budgeting:** Hard costs, soft costs, and FF&E.
- **Life-Cycle Analysis:** 10-year operation and maintenance (O&M) projections.
- **Escalation:** Compounding annual inflation adjustments for the 10-year horizon.

### 6. EVALUATION CRITERIA (100-Point Scale)

- **Technical Expertise & Experience (30%):** K-12 facility assessment and demographic modeling history.
- **Community Engagement Strategy (25%):** Quality of plan for Freeport, Durham, and Pownal outreach.
- **Project Approach (20%):** Methodology for addressing reuse, transportation, and sustainability.
- **Financial Forecasting (15%):** Accuracy in escalated budgetary estimates and life-cycle analysis.
- **Capacity & References (10%):** Ability to meet the December 2026 deadline.
