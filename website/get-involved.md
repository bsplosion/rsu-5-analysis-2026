## Sign the Withdrawal Petition

A withdrawal petition is currently circulating in Pownal. Signing the petition starts the statutory process under Maine law (§1466).

**What happens after signing:**

1. **Petition** (current step) — requires signatures from registered voters
2. **Town authorization vote** — Pownal voters decide whether to begin formal withdrawal
3. **Negotiation period** — terms of withdrawal are negotiated between Pownal and RSU 5
4. **State review** — the Maine Commissioner of Education reviews the agreement
5. **Final referendum** — every registered Pownal voter decides yes or no

Nothing is final until that last vote. But the full process must be completed by **November 2026**, and every step takes time.
