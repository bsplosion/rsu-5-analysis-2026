## Source Data

The complete dataset used in our analysis is available in the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/bsplosion/rsu-5-analysis-2026):

- [RSU 5 Budget Documents](https://github.com/bsplosion/rsu-5-analysis-2026/tree/main/data/RSU%205%20Budget%20Documents) — adopted budgets, FY22 through FY27
- [Finance Committee Minutes](https://github.com/bsplosion/rsu-5-analysis-2026/tree/main/data/RSU%205%20Finance%20Committee) — meeting minutes and recommendations
- [Board Meeting Minutes](https://github.com/bsplosion/rsu-5-analysis-2026/tree/main/data/RSU%205%20Board%20Meeting%20Minutes) — school board proceedings
- [Maine DOE Data](https://github.com/bsplosion/rsu-5-analysis-2026/tree/main/data/Maine%20DOE) — state education department filings
- [RSU 5 Presentations](https://github.com/bsplosion/rsu-5-analysis-2026/tree/main/data/RSU%205%20Presentations) — administration presentations including "Planning for the Future of RSU 5"
